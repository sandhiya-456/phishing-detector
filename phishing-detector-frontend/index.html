Phishing Safety Tips

Check HTTPS

Always ensure the website uses HTTPS and has a valid SSL certificate

Verify Domain

Check for misspellings or unusual domain names (e.g., gooogle.com instead of google.com)

Email Links

Hover over links in emails before clicking to see the actual URL

Urgency

Be wary of messages creating urgency or asking for personal information

What makes a URL suspicious?

Suspicious URLs often have unusual length, IP addresses instead of domain names, multiple subdomains, special characters like @, or misspelled brand names.
